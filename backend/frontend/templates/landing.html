Our Escort Services

Tailored support for every need, ensuring safe and compassionate companionship

Healthcare Escort

General medical appointments or consultations not tied to specific treatments.

Examples: GP visits, outpatient check-ups, hospital follow-ups

Therapy Escort

Support to physiotherapy, occupational, or counseling sessions.

Examples: PT, OT, speech therapy, mental health visits

Dialysis Escort

Reliable accompaniment for recurring dialysis schedules.

Examples: Hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis appointments

Vaccination / Check-up

Short appointments for vaccinations, tests, or wellness screenings.

Examples: Flu shots, blood tests, health screenings

Mobility Assistance

Extra help for individuals with reduced mobility during trips.

Examples: Wheelchair navigation, transfers, wayfinding

Community Event

Safe companionship to social, cultural, or community activities.

Examples: Support groups, library visits, community fairs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our escort services

How do I request an escort service?

Log in to your account and submit a request with your appointment details. Our team will match you with an available volunteer.

Are the volunteers trained and vetted?

Yes, all volunteers undergo background checks and receive safety and first aid training.

Is there a cost for the escort service?

Our services are provided by volunteers at no cost. Donations are welcome to sustain operations.
